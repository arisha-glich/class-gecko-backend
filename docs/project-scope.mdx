## **Core Business: Class/Activity Management Platform**
This appears to be a **family-oriented educational/recreational activity booking system** (like dance studios, swim schools, gymnastics centers, martial arts, or multi-activity centers).

---

## **Key Functional Areas:**

### 1. **Multi-Role User Management**
- **Families** (primary users) - book classes for their children
- **Students** - the actual participants with detailed profiles (measurements, medical info)
- **Staff** - manage operations
- **Business Owners** - run the organization
- **Members** - possibly membership-based access

### 2. **Class & Scheduling System**
- **Ongoing Classes** (recurring weekly/monthly programs)
- **Drop-in Classes** (one-time sessions)
- **Lessons** within classes (individual sessions)
- **Camps** (multi-day programs with flexible booking options)
- **Trials** - free/discounted first sessions
- **Waitlists** - for full classes

### 3. **Complex Pricing & Discounts**
- Per-lesson pricing
- **Term-based billing** (seasonal enrollment)
- **Sibling discounts** (family with multiple children)
- **Multi-enrollment discounts** (booking multiple classes)
- Registration fees & seasonal fees
- Time-limited promotional discounts
- Commission/revenue sharing system

### 4. **Booking & Enrollment Flow**
- Shopping cart system
- Trial class requests
- Waitlist management
- Class capacity limits
- Age restrictions (min/max age)
- Portal-based family bookings

### 5. **Payment & Financial Management**
- Multiple payment methods
- Refund processing
- Commission tracking (platform takes a cut)
- Tiered commission structures
- Payment terms and installments

### 6. **Family Portal Features**
- Emergency contact management
- Multiple contact persons per family
- Billing contact designation
- Photo/video consent
- Medical information storage
- Student measurements (for uniforms/equipment)

### 7. **Business Operations**
- Holiday management (class cancellations)
- Attendance tracking
- Waivers & policies
- Location management (multi-location support)
- Teacher assignments
- Custom business settings (timezone, currency, language)

### 8. **Communication System**
- Email & WhatsApp notifications
- System alerts, payment reminders
- Direct messaging to families
- Query/support ticket system

### 9. **Multi-Tenant Business Model**
- Each business has own organization settings
- Custom branding (logo, theme, colors)
- Commission-based revenue model (SaaS platform)
- Country-specific configurations

---

## **Business Model Inference:**

This is likely a **B2B SaaS platform** that provides:
- White-label booking systems for activity centers
- Family portal for self-service enrollment
- Admin dashboard for business owners
- Revenue sharing via platform commissions

**Target Customers:** Dance studios, swim schools, gymnastics centers, martial arts dojos, arts programs, sports academies, summer camps

**Key Revenue Streams:**
1. Platform commissions on bookings
2. Possible subscription fees for businesses
3. Transaction fees on payments

✅ **Family-centric** - designed for parents booking for multiple children
✅ **Flexible scheduling** - recurring, drop-in, and camp formats
✅ **Complex discounting** - sibling, multi-class, early bird
✅ **Trial management** - converting trials to paid enrollments
✅ **Waitlist automation** - filling spots when available
✅ **Multi-communication** - email + WhatsApp
✅ **Detailed student profiles** - measurements for equipment/uniforms
✅ **Term-based billing** - seasonal enrollment periods
